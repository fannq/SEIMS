calculation of sediment outflux from a cell based on a explicit solution of the time/space matrix, j = time and i = place: j1i1 is the new output, j1i is the new flux at the upstream 'entrance' flowing into the gridcell

Parameters
Qj1i1Qj+1,i+1 : result kin wave for this cell ;j = time, i = place
Qj1iQj+1,i : sum of all upstreamwater from kin wave
Qji1Qj,i+1 : incoming Q for kinematic wave (t=j) in this cell, map Qin in LISEM
Sj1iSj+1,i : sum of all upstream sediment
Sji1Si,j+1 : incoming Sed for kinematic wave (t=j) in this cell, map Qsin in LISEM
alphaalpha calculated in LISEM from before kinematic wave
dttimestep
dxdx: length of the cell, corrected for slope (DX map in LISEM)
